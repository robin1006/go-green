 "Bonsai" is a Japanese pronunciation of the earlier
Chinese term 'penzai'.
A "bon" is a tray like typically
used in bonsai culture.
This article focuses on bonsai as defined
in the Japanese tradition.
A bonsai is created begining with a specimen
of source material.
This may be a cutting, seeding, or small trees of species
suitable for bonsai development.
The purposes of bonsai are
primarily contempletion and the pleasent exercise of
effort and ingenuity.
By contrast with the other plant cultivation practices,
bonsai is not intended of production for food, for medicine and or creating yard size or park size gardens or landscapes.
