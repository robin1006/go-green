When most of people think of gardens they think of backyard flower beds,
or maybe potted plants
inside green houses. However there are actually many
different kinds of gardens,
some of which don't even need the sun, or soil. Before you start your garden
you should first decide what kind of garden you're going to grow.

Indoor

Indoor gardening is any garden
that is grown inside. This may include a few plants
on your windowsill,or a large number of crops grown
in a specialized grown room.



Outdoor

Outdoor garden often feature flower beds,
vegetable patches, or potted plants.This kind of garden is not only good
for growing a variety of crops,it can also be decorative and can add
a splash of colour to your yard.



Soil

A soil garden is simply a garden where
plants are growen in soil.They're usally found outside,
however,
they can also be used inside as well.
